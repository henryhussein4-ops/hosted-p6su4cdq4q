⌨ ZeeStudios — Shortcuts & Guide

RecordClick the big green mic button to start/stop recording. It turns red while recording.
Play / PauseSpace or click the play button.
StopS or click Stop.
Undo / RedoCtrl+Z / Ctrl+Y
Cut / Copy / PasteCtrl+X / Ctrl+C / Ctrl+V — Select a region on the waveform first by clicking and dragging.
Select regionClick and drag on the vocal waveform to select a region. Then Cut, Trim, or Delete it.
Import BeatClick "Import Beat / Music" to load any audio file from your device as a backing track.
FX ChainClick the FX button to open the effects panel. Toggle each effect on/off and adjust the knobs.
PresetsApply instant voice presets: Clean Vocal, Radio Voice, Studio Rap, Soft R&B.
ExportClick ⋮ → Export, or the download button in transport. Exports a full WAV mixdown.
MetronomeClick CLICK OFF to enable the click track. Set BPM in the box next to it.
ProjectsProjects auto-save to your browser's local storage. Use the Home screen to manage them.

Export Mix

Render the full mix (Beat + Vocals + FX) to a WAV file.
